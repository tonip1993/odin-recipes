Cannoli

Home

Description

Cannoli are Italian pastries consisting of tube-shaped shells filled with a sweet, creamy filling, often containing ricotta cheese.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup white wine or vinegar
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips or candied fruit for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt. Add butter and mix until crumbly.
  2. Add the beaten egg and wine/vinegar. Knead until smooth. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough thinly and cut into circles. Wrap around cannoli tubes and seal edges with water.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the shells until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  6. Fill the cooled shells with the ricotta mixture using a piping bag.
  7. Garnish with chocolate chips or candied fruit if desired. Serve immediately.
