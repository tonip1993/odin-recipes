Arancini

Home

Description

Arancini are Italian rice balls that are coated with breadcrumbs and fried, often filled with cheese or meat.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked risotto
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Allow the risotto to cool completely.
  2. Take a small amount of risotto in your hand, flatten it, and place a cube of mozzarella in the center. Form the risotto around the cheese into a ball.
  3. Dip each ball into flour, then into the beaten eggs, and finally coat with breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the arancini until golden brown on all sides.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
